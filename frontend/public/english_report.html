Industry Status & Pain Points

Hong Kong has over 100,000 insurance practitioners, yet their workflows are highly traditional. They rely excessively on manual processes, emails, and fragmented tools, resulting in extremely low efficiency.

Meanwhile, insurance companies have invested heavily in building traditional digital systems (Core Systems). Replacing them entirely is costly and risky.

Background & Achievements

Our Strategy: "AI Capability Extension". We integrate AI capabilities seamlessly without disrupting the original core systems, ensuring a low-cost, high-efficiency upgrade.
